Popular painting levels

Battle Ready

4-5 colors for each miniature, no shadows, no highlighted details

Standart

5-6 colors, shadows, highlights and edges, accents on the main details

Elite

6+ colors, as in Standart plus accents on all details, gradients, additional effects

How to make miniatures personal?

  • Smooth gradients will diversify sculpt
  • Elaboration of facial features and hair style create bright personality of your miniature
  • Non-standart painting schema stands out your miniature from many
  • Additional details on base will emphazise theme of the miniature
